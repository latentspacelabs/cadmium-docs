## **About Cadmium**

Cadmium was developed to help traditional animators digitally color their line frames.

## Key Features

The main processes of Cadmium are:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Import Line Artwork"
    icon="upload"
    href="/importing-frames"
  >
    Import your line artwork as numbered .png sequences with an alpha channel.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Reference Frames"
    icon="image"
    href="/reference-frames"
  >
    Import or create reference frames within Cadmium to define the color of each segment on your image.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Analyzing"
    icon="search"
    href="/analyzing-frames"
  >
    Define a "segment map" of your line image, which defines all boundaries of different areas that can be colored uniquely.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Colorizing"
    icon="palette"
    href="/colorizing"
  >
    Based on your reference image, Cadmium will make predictions about color choices for uncolored frames.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Export Your Work"
    icon="download"
    href="/exporting"
  >
    Export your completed work to be integrated back into your pipeline. Each color can be exported separately.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Drawing Tools"
    icon="pencil"
    href="/drawing-tools"
  >
    Use manual drawing tools to fine-tune your colorization and make precise adjustments.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
